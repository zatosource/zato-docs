enclog genkey
=============

Generates a new
`Fernet key <https://cryptography.io/en/latest/fernet/>`_
and outputs it on screen.

Usage
-----

::

    $ enclog genkey
    fPdM5ETGs9JValjNchbHqHHGy4WB1MSzQ ..
    $
